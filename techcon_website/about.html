Our History

TechCon began as a small meetup for open-source enthusiasts and grew into a global conference where engineers, designers, and researchers share practical ideas that move technology forward.

Over the years, TechCon has highlighted breakthroughs in web platforms, AI, accessibility, and developer tooling—always with an emphasis on collaboration and real-world impact.

Our Mission

TechCon’s mission is to democratize knowledge, promote accessible design, and empower builders at every level to create reliable, inclusive, and sustainable technology.

We curate talks and workshops that turn complex topics into clear, actionable steps you can use immediately in your projects.
